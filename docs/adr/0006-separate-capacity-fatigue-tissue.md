# Separate capacity, fatigue, and tissue stress

Long-horizon capacities (`X`), short-horizon fatigues (`F`), and regional tissue
stress (`T`) are stored separately. A strong tired athlete is not the same as a weak
fresh athlete, and a globally fresh athlete with localized knee/lumbar tissue stress
still needs constraints. Collapsing these into a single fitness-only or fatigue-only
score destroys exactly the distinctions the prescriber depends on.

**Guardrail:** do not reduce the engine to either fitness-only or fatigue-only logic.
